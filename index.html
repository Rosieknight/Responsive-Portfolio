About me

Hi, my name is Laura. I have a Bachelor's from UCF in History, and an AA in Business from SSC. I currently work in retail. At this point in my life, I am more than ready for a change in careers.

I'm a Star Wars fan, though my relationship with the later books is complicated. I love to read, especially mysteries, fantasy, and science fiction. I don't have a consistent favorite book. It tends to vary based on my mood and current reading habits. There are a handful of books and stories I will frequently recommend, if asked. My recommendations really depends on what genre you want to read.

I enjoy a wide variety of music, ranging from bands like the Beatles or Led Zeppelin to movie scores and soundtracks. There are a handful of songs that I consider my favorite, though these are also subject to change with mood and current listening preferences. Some of my consistent favorites include Classical Gas by Mannheim Steamroller, Let It Be by the Beatles, and the Star Wars Main Theme.
